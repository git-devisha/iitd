Framework of Metrics
Precision In Every Measure, Excellence In Every Outcome
A novel framework of metrics to assess prototypes is proposed to assist design practitioners and researchers in choosing relevant metrics from an encompassing list based on the selection criteria of kind of prototype, prototyping tool, and evaluation category. This framework caters to the need for support to equip practitioners and researchers with a comprehensive list of metrics from which they can choose relevant ones.
